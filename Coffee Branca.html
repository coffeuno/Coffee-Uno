Coffee Branca is regarded as an introductory coffee beverage.

The mild bitterness of Brazilian Bourbon Santos Coffee beans were cut by the amount of milk in the beverage. It has a rich mouthfeel that coats the inside of your lips and tastes like a milky coffee with a somewhat milder flavor than some other similar drinks such as the Cappuccino. We named it Coffee Branca because the beans used by Coffee Uno are from Brazil, hence we used the Portuguese word "Branca," which means "white. Coffee branca is the name of our creamy white coffee variant.
Ingredients:
Coffee creamer, Hydrogenerated vegetable oil, sugar, instant coffee, iodized salt, flavor sweetener, anti-caking agent, modified milk
